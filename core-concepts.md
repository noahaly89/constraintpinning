# Core Concepts

## Constraint Object
A structured and versioned representation of a requirement.

## Constraint Registry
The authoritative store for approved constraints, their versions, ownership, and status.

## Constraint Contract
A named package of constraints assigned to an agent, workflow, tool, or environment.

## Pinning
The binding of a constraint or contract to a governed execution scope.

## Validator
A deterministic or policy-assisted component that evaluates an intended action.

## Enforcement
The response to a validation result: allow, warn, require approval, transform, or block.

## Evidence Record
An immutable record of the evaluated action, applicable constraint version, result, decision, and timestamp.
